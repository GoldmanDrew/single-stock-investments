## Executive summary

Amazon remains a **multi-engine compounder** (retail logistics, AWS, advertising, custom silicon) with **stable moat** and **partial dhando**. Decision-stack refresh at **~$263** (2026-05-25): base **3.6%** 10yr IRR on **normalized** owner cash flow **$5.35/sh** (not spot TTM FCF). Marvin proposes **watch** — exceptional business, sub-15% return at price until capex normalizes or entry improves.
