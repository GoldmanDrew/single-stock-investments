## Executive summary

Consensus Mining & Seigniorage (CMSG, OTCQX) is a **Horizon-affiliated digital-asset miner** whose economics split between thin **operating mining** (~$4.5M FY2025 revenue, ~$1.4M operating loss) and a **large treasury** (~$60.5M cash + ~$31.3M fair-value crypto at YE2025). GAAP earnings are dominated by **ASU 2023-08 fair-value marks**, not hash cash flow. At **~$30**, base **scenario IRR 7.7%** fails the 15% bar; Marvin proposes **watch** (moat unproven, partial dhando). Upside is a **BTC/treasury re-rating**; primary risk is **continued network-share loss** and related-party fee drag post-waiver.
