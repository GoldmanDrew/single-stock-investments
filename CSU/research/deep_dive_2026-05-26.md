## What this business is

Constellation Software Inc. is a Canadian holding company that acquires and holds vertical market software (VMS) businesses. **[Fact]** Customers are niche industry users paying maintenance and license fees on mission-critical software. **[Fact]** Cash is generated as **free cash flow available to shareholders (FCFA2S)** and redeployed almost entirely into new acquisitions at hurdle rates. **[Fact]** FY2025 revenue was **$11.62B** (+15%); FCFA2S **$1.68B** (+14%). **[Fact]** Q1 2026 revenue **$3.18B** (+20%) with **6%** organic growth. **[Inference]** GAAP net income is a poor owner metric because earnout fair-value swings distort reported earnings.

---

## Why the market might be wrong

There is **no clear dated mispricing signal** (HK predictive attribute: **none**). **[Inference]** Generalists focus on GAAP net income volatility (FY2025 NI **$586M**, down 24% YoY) and law-of-large-numbers concerns, while the owner metric **FCFA2S (~$79.5/sh)** compounds steadily. Return is **serial acquirer reinvestment at price**: the question is whether hurdle-rate deals remain available as scale grows, not whether a hidden asset is marked wrong tomorrow.

Hohn screen: **pass** on base upside; downside **partial** (premium serial-acquirer multiple).

**Returns statement:** We expect about **16%** per year over 10 years from **FCFA2S growth** and a sustained serial-acquirer premium multiple. Primary risk: **paying too much for acquisitions** as consolidated scale rises.
