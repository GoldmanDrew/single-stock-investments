## Executive summary

Mesabi Trust is a passive royalty trust holding leasehold interests tied to Cleveland-Cliffs’ Northshore iron ore mining and pellet operations in Minnesota. Cash available to owners is entirely a function of pellet tons shipped/priced out of Silver Bay under long-dated leases, minus trust expenses (including arbitration-related legal fees) and any reserve build the Trustees deem prudent.

After a multi-year royalty dispute with Cliffs/Northshore, Mesabi booked **$71.185 million** of arbitration-award revenue in the fiscal period ended October 31, 2024 (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-Q_20251212_rpt20251031_acc0001104659_25_120673.htm`, Condensed Statements of Income section), which dramatically distorted year-over-year comparisons. Excluding that one-time item, fiscal 2026 nine-month **royalty income** was **$13,156,985** vs **$19,613,693** in the prior-year nine months (same filing), as the Trust cites lower pricing, lower shipments, Feb 2025 extended maintenance, and a weaker bonus royalty tier.

The operator relationship remains adversarial: the Form 10-K filed April 22, 2026 discloses **AAA arbitration** commenced September 26, 2025 relating to NMC idling May 2022–April 2023 and **underpayment on intercompany shipments** from 2023 through the present (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-K_20260422_rpt20260131_acc0001104659_26_046864.htm`, Item 3). Bonus royalty visibility is thin: the May 4, 2026 press release describes **$0 bonus royalty** for CQ1 2026 with deemed prices drawn from a sparse third-party sale in September 2025 (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/8-K_20260504_exhibit_msb-20260430xex99d1.htm_acc0001104659_26_054941.htm`).

Approved HK memory already flags **royalty trusts and K-1 yield-screen exclusions** as a persistent market-structure discount (`_system/memory/MEMORY.md`). The open question for owners is whether distributions over time reflect durable economic rent on high-grade pellets under contract, or episodic litigation recoveries and volatile Cliffs strategy.

## 1. Facts (from primary filings)

### Structure and listing

- Mesabi Trust holds **units of beneficial interest** listed on the **NYSE** under **MSB**; **13,120,010** units outstanding as of April 21, 2026; non-accelerated filer / smaller reporting company (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-K_20260422_rpt20260131_acc0001104659_26_046864.htm`, cover / Item 5 table of contents region).
- No employees; Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas is **Corporate Trustee**; four individual Trustees oversee a pass-through entity whose activities are limited to collecting income, paying liabilities, and distributing net cash (`10-K` cited above, Item 10 narrative).
- Corporate Trustee compensation can scale with **0.25% of trust moneys distributed** (in addition to escalated fixed fees) — material at high payout levels (`10-K` cited above, Item 11; figures include **$316,442** total Corporate Trustee compensation for fiscal year ended Jan 31, 2026).

### Economic engine

- Essentially **one segment — iron ore mining royalties** (`10-K` cited above, Item 1(b)).
- Royalty mechanics (escalating tiers, bonus royalty linked to price thresholds, price true-ups tied to Cliffs’ customer contracts outside the Trust’s control) are summarized under **Item 2** and **Notes** in the Form 10-Q for the quarter ended October 31, 2025 (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-Q_20251212_rpt20251031_acc0001104659_25_120673.htm`, Item 2 — Trustees’ Discussion).

- **Sept 26, 2025**: Mesabi commenced **AAA arbitration** against Northshore & Cliffs seeking damages / declaratory relief for idling May 2022–Apr 2023 and alleged **underpayment on intercompany shipments** from 2023–present (`10-K` Jan 31, 2026, Item 3).

### Controls / dependence on operator data

Northshore declines written SOX-style certification to the trustees; trustees rely on **annual operator certification**, outside accountants, and **Eveleth Fee Office** mine observations / scale checks (`10-K` Jan 31, 2026, Item 9A).

## 2. Inferences (supported, but require judgment)

1. **Run-rate cash economics are currently nearer the “nine-month royalty income ~$13.2M” neighborhood** than the litigation-inflated TTM EPS figures that include the **$71.185M** award recognition in the base period (`10-Q` Oct 31, 2025).
2. **Bonus royalty volatility is acute** — a $0 bonus quarter in CQ1 2026 (per May 2026 release) underscores dependence on crossing the **$71.70/ton** (2026 threshold cited in release) referenced prices and arms-length sale evidence.
3. **Operator strategic uncertainty** is a first-order variable: trustees cite lack of clarity on **Cliffs'** vertical integration / Northshore throughput plans (`8-K` Apr 14, 2026 press release).
4. **Intercompany shipment pricing remains a contested cash-flow lever**, separate from headline pellet indices, reinforcing legal tail risk alongside commodity cycle risk (`10-K` Item 3; HK Q3 2025 commentary narrative on **Cliffs'** royalty interpretation — `_system/reference/investment-wisdom/horizon-kinetics/HK-Q3-2025-Commentary-extract.txt`).

## 3. Opinions (analysis — not facts)

### Munger lenses (approved memory applied)

**Inversion:** Failure modes include prolonged low bonus tiers, hostile operator interpretation of premium pellet economics, permitting or idle events at Northshore, and negative price adjustments that claw back accruals (Trust highlights these classes of risks in quarterly forward-looking language: `10-Q` Oct 31, 2025, opening risk paragraph).

**Psychology:** Market participants may anchor to arbitration windfalls or yield screens rather than stripping out one-time arbitration revenue and stressing Cliffs/Northshore path.

### Pabrai (dhando)

The structure can be dhando-like **only at a sufficiently conservative price** relative to pessimistic pellet shipment/pricing assumptions and allowance for recurring legal expense — tails include total loss of bonus tier for extended stretches and recurrence of covenant-like disputes (`MSB` filings above). **Dhando labeled `partial`** — upside tied to contractual rent; downside includes operator hostility and capex/strategy shocks.

### Stahl / portfolio context

Treat MSB weight as exposure to **one mine’s pellet chain economics**, not diversification — consistent with `_system/memory/MEMORY.md` guidance on superficial index diversification.

### Horizon Kinetics memory (explicit)

Royalty trusts sit alongside **persistent market-structure discounts** (`_system/memory/MEMORY.md`, HK bullets). Earlier HK commentary juxtaposed Mesabi with **temporary dividend disruptions** resolving over multi-year arcs (`_system/reference/investment-wisdom/horizon-kinetics/HK-Q1-2025-Commentary-extract.txt`, Mesabi / SJT comparison excerpt). HK Q3 2025 also describes **Cliffs'** contested **premium pellet royalty** framing and arbitration (`_system/reference/investment-wisdom/horizon-kinetics/HK-Q3-2025-Commentary-extract.txt`, Mesabi section).
