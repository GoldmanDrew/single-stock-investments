## What this business is

Mesabi Trust is a passive royalty trust that receives iron ore pellet production payments from Cleveland-Cliffs' Northshore Mining operation in Silver Bay, Minnesota. **[Fact]** The Trust has no employees and no operating business; cash flows are tons shipped times realized pricing times bonus tier formulas. **[Fact]** There are 13,120,010 units outstanding. **[Fact]** Calendar Q1 2026 shipments were 938,572 tons (+105% YoY) but bonus royalty was **$0** (first zero-bonus outcome in years except Covid idling); total Q1 royalty was $1.63 million. **[Fact]** Calendar Q4 2025 payment was $4.9 million including $1.04 million bonus. AAA arbitration commenced September 26, 2025 on idling and intercompany pricing disputes.

---

## Why the market might be wrong

The market may annualize Q1 2026's zero bonus and treat Mesabi like a broken income vehicle, missing the Horizon Kinetics transitory-problem framing. **[Inference]** Bonus tiers are mechanical: when deemed pellet prices sit below the adjusted threshold ($71.70/ton for 2026), bonus drops to zero even as tonnage recovers. **[Inference]** Yield screens exclude royalty trusts; decades of ~9.8% forty-year total return are invisible to index buyers. Normalized distribution yield at ~$26 exceeds 8% on >$2/unit per year mid-cycle, not on depressed Q1 alone.

## Executive summary

Mesabi Trust is a contractual pellet royalty on Cliffs' Northshore mine with cyclical commodity exposure and a legal recovery option. Q1 2026 bonus royalty was zero despite tonnage doubling YoY; Q4 2025 still paid $1.04 million bonus, showing mechanics intact. At $25.68 (Stooq, May 26, 2026), normalized distribution yield is ~8.4% (~$2.16/unit per year); equity-yield-curve base is ~10.3% over five years; bull ~16.5%. September 2025 arbitration adds a dated catalyst. Dhando is partial with a strong passive-trust floor. We propose hold for the incumbent royalty sleeve, not watch on depressed-Q1 Lawrence math alone.
