## Executive summary

OTC Markets Group is a **Stahl croupier**: regulated U.S. OTC equity markets (OTCQX, OTCQB, OTCID, Pink) plus FINRA-member ATS infrastructure (OTC Link, extended hours). Revenue is **subscription- and market-data-heavy** with limited principal risk on core operations.

**FY2025:** Net revenues **$107,882K**; diluted EPS **$2.58** (+14% YoY). **Q1 2026:** Net revenues **$33,842K** (+15% YoY); diluted EPS **$0.59** (+18% vs prior-year quarter). ~**11.86M** diluted shares.

Decision-stack refresh at **~$52.11**: base **9.7%** 10yr IRR — **below 15% hold band**. Stance proposal: **watch** (quality niche croupier, not a fat pitch at ~22× run-rate earnings).
