## What this business is

S&P Global sells financial intelligence: credit ratings on debt issuance, index licensing tied to benchmark AUM, Market Intelligence data subscriptions, Platts commodity benchmarks, and Mobility analytics (spin planned mid-2026). **[Fact]** Customers pay transaction fees when markets issue debt, recurring subscriptions for data, and AUM-linked index fees. **[Fact]** FY2025 revenue was $15.336 billion (+8%); adjusted diluted EPS was $17.83 (+14%). Q1 2026 revenue was $4.171 billion (+10% organic) with adjusted operating margin 51.8%. The business earns toll-like fees with minimal principal risk on core operations.

## Why the market might be wrong

No clear mispricing signal or dated payoff. **[Inference]** Quality compounders rarely trade to a 15% IRR; the market prices covenant-required ratings and S&P 500 index entrenchment at a premium. **[Inference]** May 2026 Market Intelligence leadership transition may create temporary concern, but 2026 guidance was reiterated. Return is franchise earnings at price unless an issuance drought forces a cyclical re-rating.
