## Executive summary

Teqnion AB is a **Swedish industrial serial acquirer** (listed First North 2019) owning 30+ decentralized niche-industrial subsidiaries. The 2025–2026 **margin and FCF turnaround** is visible — FY2025 EBITA +36%, Q1 2026 EBITA margin **14.3%** (record) — while **organic net sales remain −5%** from deliberate pruning of unprofitable units. At **~158 SEK**, base **13.7%** 10yr IRR on FY2025 FCF/sh sits **below the 15% hold band**; Marvin proposes **watch** (quality compounder, not a fat pitch at this price). Thesis **strengthening** on operating proof; M&A discipline is the swing factor.
