## Investment Thesis Summary (from public sources)

**Business model:** Serial acquirer of small industrial niche companies (SEK 25–150M revenue), decentralized management, eternal holding period.

**Comparable:** Often compared to early-stage Lagercrantz — similar decentralized industrial conglomerate model.

**Key metrics cited:**
- Group ROIC consistently >20%
- ~80% of acquisitions meet 5-year payback hurdle
- 10-year EPS CAGR ~23% (through 2025)
- Largest shareholder: Antheia AB (Daniel Ek's family office), ~12%

**Key risks cited:**
- Profitability turnaround still in progress (CEO self-graded "D-" for 2025)
- ~1/3 of subsidiaries loss-making in weak macro
- Goodwill impairments (73 MSEK in Q3 2025)
- Acquisition pipeline depends on small deal team
