## Executive summary

WaterBridge Infrastructure operates **produced-water gathering, disposal, and recycling** in the Delaware Basin (~**2,500 miles** of pipe) under long-term fee contracts with E&P operators. Post-IPO (Sep 2025) combination scale drives strong growth: FY2025 revenue **$525.6M** (+66%), Adj. EBITDA **$254.0M** (+68%); Q1 2026 revenue **$201M** (+105%), Adj. EBITDA **$102.9M** (+105%), produced-water volumes **2,460 MBbl/d** (+105%). **Not a Stahl croupier** — asset-heavy merchant infrastructure with **volume cyclicality**, **$1.47B debt**, and **Five Point sponsor control**. At **~$31**, base **12.1%** 10yr IRR on maintenance-adjusted FCF/sh is **below 15%**; moat **unproven** and dhando **pending** → Marvin proposes **watch**.
