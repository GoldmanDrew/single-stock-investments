Mesabi receives royalties on pellets shipped/priced out of Silver Bay, Minnesota under long-lived leases and passes through cash net of trustee costs and Trustee-discretionary reserves.

**Facts that matter**

- Litigation economics dominated comparisons: FY25 window included recognizing **$71.185 million** arbitration award revenue in the quarter ended October 31, 2024 (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-Q_20251212_rpt20251031_acc0001104659_25_120673.htm`).
- Fiscal 2026 nine-month **royalty income** (through Oct 31, 2025) was **$13,156,985** versus **$19,613,693** YoY absent a repeat arbitration inflow (`10-Q` cited above).
- **New AAA arbitration** filed Sept 2025 alleging damages for NMC idling (May 2022–Apr 2023) and intercompany shipment underpayments from 2023–present (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/10-K_20260422_rpt20260131_acc0001104659_26_046864.htm` Item 3).
- **Apr 2026** distribution lowered to **$0.24/unit** versus **$0.56** a year earlier; trustees cite lower January 2026 royalty receipt (**$4.94M vs $8.99M**) and reserve posture (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/8-K_20260414_exhibit_msb-20260414xex99d1.htm_acc0001104659_26_043216.htm`).
- **CQ1 2026** operator report showed **bonus royalty $0** and sparse third-party reference transactions (`MSB/investor-documents/sec-edgar/8-K_20260504_exhibit_msb-20260430xex99d1.htm_acc0001104659_26_054941.htm`).
